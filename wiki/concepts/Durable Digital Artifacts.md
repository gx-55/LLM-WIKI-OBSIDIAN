# Durable Digital Artifacts

Durable digital artifacts are digital works that remain accessible beyond the
lifespan of a particular app, service, company, or platform. In [[File Over App]],
durability comes from user-controlled files in formats that are easy to retrieve
and read.

## Risks To Durability

- Cloud-only storage
- Login-gated services
- Opaque databases
- Proprietary formats
- Tools that make export difficult or incomplete

## Practical Pattern

Plain text and other open formats are durable because they can be read by many
tools, including tools from older or future computing environments. This is why
markdown-based vaults pair naturally with [[Compounding Knowledge Base]] and
[[LLM Wiki Pattern]] workflows.
