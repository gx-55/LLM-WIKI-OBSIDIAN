# Developer Second Brain

A Developer Second Brain is the engineering side of a personal knowledge vault:
ADRs, debriefs, projects, snippets, technical reading notes, and related
decision history. It complements the [[LLM Wiki Pattern]] by preserving not only
what the developer has read, but what the developer has decided and learned
through work.

## Design Pressure

Keeping developer artifacts in a separate vault loses connections between
research and decisions. Merging them without discipline creates clutter. The
article's answer is [[Vault Zone Separation]], with `dev/` as the collaborative
zone where the human drives and the agent assists.
