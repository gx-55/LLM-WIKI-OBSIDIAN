# Compounding Knowledge Base

A compounding knowledge base accumulates value as each source, query, and
synthesis leaves durable structure behind. In the [[LLM Wiki Pattern]], this
means the wiki becomes richer every time the agent updates summaries, adds
links, records contradictions, or creates new concept pages.

## Contrast With Retrieval

Retrieval-oriented systems often answer from raw documents at query time. That
can work for isolated questions, but subtle synthesis may be repeated again and
again. A compounding knowledge base makes the synthesis itself a persistent
artifact.

## Maintenance Work

The hard part is not only reading sources. It is keeping cross-references,
indices, entity pages, and evolving claims aligned. The pattern depends on the
LLM doing this maintenance continuously.
